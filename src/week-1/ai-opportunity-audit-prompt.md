# My AI Opportunity Analysis

## Context: What Makes a Great AI Opportunity

Strong AI opportunities have these characteristics:
- **Frequent**: Done weekly or more often
- **Time-consuming**: Takes significant time each occurrence
- **Frustrating**: Drains energy or feels like "there must be a better way"
- **Repeatable logic**: Even if the content varies, the process is consistent
- **Information-heavy**: Requires research, synthesis, or pulling from multiple sources
- **Clear quality criteria**: You know what "good" looks like
