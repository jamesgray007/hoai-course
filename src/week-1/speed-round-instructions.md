## How to Complete the Speed Round

For each of the 10 questions, you'll provide **four pieces of information**:

### 1. **Workflow** (5-15 words)
Short, specific description of the task.

✅ Good: "Preparing weekly leadership meeting agendas"  
❌ Too vague: "Meetings"

### 2. **Frequency**
How often you do this. Type exactly:
- **Daily** (5+ times per week)
- **Weekly** (1-4 times per week)
- **Monthly** (less than weekly)

### 3. **Time** (hours per week)
Realistic estimate including prep, execution, cleanup.

**Examples:**
- Quick tasks: 0.5-2 hours
- Moderate tasks: 2-5 hours
- Major tasks: 5+ hours

### 4. **Frustration** (1-10 scale)
How draining is this task?

**1-3** = Mild annoyance  
**4-6** = Moderately draining  
**7-9** = Significantly frustrating  
**10** = Excruciating

---

**Total time: 15-20 minutes for all 10 workflows**

**Pro tip:** Don't overthink the numbers. First instinct is usually right. You're looking for patterns, not precision.
