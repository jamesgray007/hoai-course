## How to Answer the Speed Round

**Format:** Short, specific phrases or sentences (5-15 words per answer)

Think: *"What would I tell a colleague if they asked what's eating my time?"*

### ‚úÖ Good Examples:

- "Preparing weekly leadership meeting agendas and action items"
- "Responding to customer contract redline requests"  
- "Building monthly performance dashboards for the executive team"
- "Researching competitive intelligence for sales proposals"

### ‚ùå What to Avoid:

- **Too vague:** "Meetings" or "Reports" or "Admin stuff"
  - *Why it's bad:* AI can't score what it can't understand
  
- **Too long:** Multi-sentence explanations with full context
  - *Why it's bad:* Buries the core task in narrative

- **Multiple tasks:** Listing 3-4 different things per answer
  - *Why it's bad:* Pick the most painful ONE

### Pro Tips:

- **First instinct is usually right** - don't overthink it
- **Be honest about pain** - this is for you, not your boss
- **Specific > Generic** - "Board deck creation" beats "presentations"
- **Time limit: 5-10 minutes** for all 10 questions

## Troubleshooting & FAQs

### Q: My AI's score for one task seems off

**A:** Reply to your AI in the same chat:

*"The score for [task name] seems [too high/low]. Here's more context: [add details about frequency/time/frustration]. Please re-score."*

Your AI will adjust based on your clarification.

---

### Q: I have more than 10 workflows I want to analyze

**A:** Start with these 10 most painful ones. After posting your Top 3, you can:
- Run the prompt again with 10 additional tasks
- Or just add them to the conversation: *"Here are 5 more workflows: [list]. Please score these too."*

---

### Q: Which AI tool should I use?

**A:** Use whichever you have access to or prefer:

- **Claude** - Tends to give the most thorough, nuanced analysis. Great for complex workflows.
- **ChatGPT** - Excellent results, slightly more concise. Good if you prefer faster responses.
- **Gemini** - Works well, especially if you're in the Google ecosystem.
- **Microsoft Copilot** - Convenient if you're already using Microsoft 365.

**All work with this prompt.** The scoring and ranking will be similar across platforms.

---

### Q: The output format doesn't exactly match what was promised

**A:** Different AI models may format results slightly differently, but all should provide:
- Scored workflows
- Ranked list
- Top 3 analysis

If something's missing, just ask: *"Can you also provide [the missing element]?"*

---

### Q: What if I don't have 10 painful workflows?

**A:** That's rare for executives, but if you truly don't have 10:
- Answer as many as you can (minimum 5)
- Think about tasks you avoid or delay (those count too)
- Consider work you WISH you had time to do (expansion opportunities)

---

### Q: Should I include workflows my team does?

**A:** For this audit, focus on **YOUR personal workflows**. 

However, if you're frequently reviewing, approving, or editing your team's work‚Äîthat counts as YOUR workflow. Example: *"Reviewing and editing team's client proposals"* is a valid answer.

---

### Q: Can I use the same AI conversation to analyze additional workflows later?

**A:** Yes! Your AI maintains context within the same chat. You can add: *"Here are 3 more workflows I forgot: [list]. Please score and rank these with the others."*
